# Review Management

Handles customer reviews, AI-generated reply suggestions, and multi-platform aggregation.

## Language

**Bewertung** (Review):
A customer review on Google (later Facebook, Apple, etc.) consisting of stars (1–5), optional text, author name, and timestamp. In German, the canonical term is _Bewertung_.
_Avoid_: Rating, Rezension, Feedback

**Antwort** (Reply):
The business's response to a Bewertung. Written by the business owner (or generated by AI and approved by the owner). Appears publicly on Google below the Bewertung.
_Avoid_: Response, Reaktion, Kommentar

**KI-Vorschlag** (AI Suggestion):
An AI-generated reply draft produced by DeepSeek. The user can choose from 2–3 Varianten (variants), edit the text, and either send it or discard it. Must be human-reviewed before sending.
_Avoid_: Draft, Variant, Generation, Suggestion

**Eingang** (Inbox):
The chronological list of all Bewertungen for a Standort, filterable by stars, answered/unanswered, and date. Future: aggregates reviews from multiple platforms in one view.
_Avoid_: Feed, Stream, List

**Sprache** (Language):
The language of the Bewertung text, detected heuristically (DE/FR/IT/EN). Used to set the language of the KI-Vorschlag, independent of the user's UI language.
_Avoid_: Locale, Tongue
